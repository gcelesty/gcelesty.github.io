Events

City Council Public Session

City council meetings are conducted each week on Tuesdays @ 1:30 PM and Wednesdays beginning at 9:00 AM. Any citizen may speak before before council on Tuesday beginning at 2:00 PM. To reserve time to address City Council, contact City Secretary's Office prior to the meeting. All Council meetings are open to the public. Meeting end times may vary.

Budget & Fiscal Affairs (BFA) Committee Meeting

The Budget and Fiscal Affairs (BFA) Committee reviews Finance, Human Resources, Office of Business Opportunity and Fleet Managemant. Committee agendas and presentations can be found online. Meeting end times may vary.

Spotlights for Best Companies to Work at

Occidental Petroleum

Occidental Petroleum Corporation is an American company engaged in hydrocarbon exploration in the United States, the Middle East, and Colombia as well as petrochemical manufacturing in the United States, Canada, and Chile. They have a great employee retention with staff members usually staying with the company for 4.7 years. Average employee here makes $81,778 per year.

ConocoPhillips

ConocoPhillips has a time-honored tradition of placing safety, health, and environmental stewardship at the top of their operating priorities. They are commited to the efficient and effective exploration and production of oil and natural gas. They have a great employee retention with staff members usually staying with the company for 5.7 years. Average employee here makes $104,408 per year.

Bracewell LLP

Bracewell LLP is an international law firm based in Houston, Texas that began in 1945. The company loves to hire graduates from the University of Houston. Bracewell is a great place to work and is featured as number 3 on Zippia's list of best companies to work at in Houston. The average employee here makes $84,249 per year.
